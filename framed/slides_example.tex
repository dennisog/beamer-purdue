  The continuous-time Fourier Transform of a signal $x(t)$ is defined as

  \begin{IEEEeqnarray}{rCl}
    \label{eq:ft}
    X(\omega) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} x(t)e^{-j\omega t}\ \mathrm{dt}
  \end{IEEEeqnarray}
